Avilash Cramer

avilash@mit.edu

A medical engineering PhD student at MIT, Avilash's research is focused on the electrical and mechanical design of x-ray hardware. In particular, he is interested in improving trauma care in remote and low-income communities. Avilash is a former wilderness EMT, and a 2014-2015 Fulbright scholar in rural India. He graduated with honors in physics from Brown University in 2014.
When not in lab, Avilash can usually be found climbing.
